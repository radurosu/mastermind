Below is an applet of the Game MasterMind ....good luck playing it.

MasterMind is one of those brain intensive little games that I played while was a little boy. The game can be played in many ways, the most common of which I have programmed here. One playing this game on the computer is commonly refered to as the Mind, while the computer plays the Master. As the mind, your purpose is to find the four peggs put in by the computer in the secret box at the bottom of the board.
